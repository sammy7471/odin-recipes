Tacos

Description

Crispy potato tacos made simple in the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (4 ounce) package instant mashed potatoes
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • 1 serving nonstick cooking spray
  • ½ cup salsa verde
  • ¼ cup crumbled cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Heat water in a saucepan. Mix with a fork to moisten all potatoes and let stand 5 minutes. Stir in Cheddar cheese, green onion.
  2. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Wrap tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave on high until warm.
  4. Put a tablespoon potato mixture in the tortilla and fold over to make a taco. Repeat
  5. place tacos in the basket of an air fryer. Spray the tops with cooking spray and cook until crispy,Transfer to a platter and repeat.
  6. Drizzle salsa verde over tacos and top with cotija cheese.
