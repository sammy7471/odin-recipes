Baked-Chicken

Description

Crispy baked chicken legs. This is easy.

Ingredients

  • chicken legs
  • chili powder
  • tablespoon garlic powder
  • cayenne pepper
  • Himalayan pink salt
  • tamari

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Place chicken legs in a large bowl. Spread chili powder, garlic powder, cayenne, and salt onto the chicken; pour sauce over top. Mix and transfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink at the bone
