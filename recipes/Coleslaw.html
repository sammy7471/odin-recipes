Coleslaw

Description

Adding a little orange juice to make the dressing a little sweeter.

This recipe easily makes enough for 10 people or more. If it's on the thin side, add another 1/2 cup of mayonnaise. If it's too thick, thin with more orange juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon orange juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 ½ heads cabbage, shredded
  • 2 carrots, shredded

Steps

  1. Stir mayonnaise, sour cream, sugar, white vinegar, orange juice, and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Toss cabbage and carrots together in a large bowl. Drizzle dressing over the cabbage mixture and toss to coat.
